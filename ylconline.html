Privacy Statement

Your privacy is important to us. The Youth Leadership Camp 2026 Team is committed to protecting the personal information you provide in this registration form. Your data will be collected, stored, and processed solely for camp administration, including but not limited to:

  • Verifying your identity and registration status
  • Communicating important camp details and updates
  • Assigning accommodations, meals, and workshop sessions
  • Addressing health and safety concerns
  • Processing payments and ensuring proper documentation

Your information will be handled with the highest level of confidentiality and will only be accessed by authorized camp organizers. We will not share, sell, or distribute your personal details to third parties without your explicit consent, except in cases where legal obligations or emergency situations require disclosure (e.g., medical emergencies).

For participants under 18 years old, a Minor Consent Form must be completed by a parent or guardian to authorize the collection and use of personal data. Any health-related details you provide will be used exclusively to ensure appropriate medical attention and accommodations when necessary.

By proceeding with this registration, you acknowledge that you have read and understood this Privacy Statement and consent to the collection and processing of your personal information for YLC 2025 purposes. If you have any concerns or wish to request modifications to your data, you may contact the YLC 2025 Registration Team.
