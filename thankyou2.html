Upload Your Payment Receipt

Please follow the instructions below to upload your payment receipt.

📱 Using iPhone:
1. Open your Camera app.
2. Point it at the QR Code below.
3. Tap the link that appears on the screen.
4. Upload your payment receipt on the website.

📱 Using Android:
1. Open your Camera or Phone Scanner app.
2. Scan the QR Code below.
3. Tap the link that appears.
4. Upload your receipt on the website.

If your phone has no scanner:
Download a QR Scanner from the Google Play Store and scan the code.
